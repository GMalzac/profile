Journease.online

Have you ever been on a trip exploring your mailbox to retrieve your plane tickets, the address of next accommodation booked on Airbnb or wondering which activity comes next? Not really funny, right?

Journease helps you store all your travel information in one place. No more mailbox exploring, just let the app guide you to your next milestone. Cherry on the cake: you can also use it as group to divid the trip planning workload!

I designed and built this MVP in 2 weeks within a team of 3.
